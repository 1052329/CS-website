Computer Science

The class is mainly focused on coding, which can be used in various ways in modern technology. The Software & Systems Development CTE Pathway offered at Anaheim High School teaches students how to program in various languages such as Python, HTML, and Javascript. Learning to code can open many doors and teach very valuable life long skills since the future is based on technology.

Enrolling in the Software & Systems Development CTE Pathway opened many doors such as being involved in the AUHSD Robotics VEX Competition at STEAM-A-POOLZA. This was a wonderful hands-on learning experience where students challenged their coding and collaboration skills to create useful machines.
